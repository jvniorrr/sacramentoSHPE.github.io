Executive Positions

Diego Ruiz

Running for: President

Diego Ruiz, President

My name is Diego Ruiz, your current Director of Events and I am running to be your President! I’ve been a part of SHPE my entire college career and want to help lead it into greater heights. My plans involve using the foundation Maria has built and developing it further. I want my term to primarily focus on you, our members, our familia.

My primary goals for my term include:
Solidifying an effective and efficient team strategy with our officers
The board is a team, one that supports each other while knowing what each individuals job is. I want to make sure each officer knows exactly what their duties are, while still having the freedom to run their position as they see fit. I will simply be a guide for them, making sure they stay on track. Our team however, is also a family. We need to make sure we’re there for each other if ones job gets tough. I will ensure that each officer has a support network and that no one person is alone in their position. Together I want to create a strategic framework to work with one another to get as much work done as possible.

Creating opportunities for member involvement within the board
Most members don’t know that our officer meetings are open for you to attend. I want to make sure next year that every one of you know this and become more involved with us so we can make SHPE what you want and give you a voice in the internal workings of the board.

Continue bringing more projects to members
Experience is everything in the engineering world, so I want to help you prepare for it by continuing to provide projects for you to gain the skillsets that are searched for throughout companies. Whether it be company tours or projects such as EEV, we will give you as many opportunities to develop the skills needed for the engineering world.

Increase collaborations with other on campus clubs to improve networking
Collaborations bring networking which in turn bring opportunities. I plan to speak with leaders of other clubs and increase our involvement with them. Different clubs bring opportunities for projects, jobs, and just friends; each of which are essential to an engineer.
“Give back more than what you take” -Dr. Victor B. Olivares
