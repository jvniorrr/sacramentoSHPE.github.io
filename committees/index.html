Join a Committee!!

Future's Committee | Vice President

The Future's Committe is a self run committee in which members grow into leaders. The VP will watch over the committee for the first few weeks to help get it started running and the committee will need to assign positions/ responsibilities to all members. The committee will be given a date to host an event that covers any of the 6 skillsets for the chapter. Time of meetings will be decided once the committee is formed.

Join The Future Committe!

Event Committee | Director of Events

The committee’s job is to help the Director of Events, Luis Magana, in planning SHPE’s events.

Join The Event Committee!

Fundraising Committee | Director of Fundraising

Fundraising is about getting as much funds as we can to sustain our chapter. Therefore, the job of this committee is to come up with fundraising strategies and organize fundraising events. My plan for the following semester is to host a couple of fundraisers as well as other activities that will get us some money. In my committee you will have the opportunity to develop leadership and communication skills. My goal is to give members what they need to grow professionally. We will be in charge of planning, organizing, and advertising fundraising events. So if you feel like you want to be part of this, please join my committee. And if you have other ideas on how to raise money please share those with me. I’d love to hear them.

Join the Fundraising Committee!

Historian Committee | Historian Chair

If you are looking for a fun and energetic committee to join, then join the Historian's Committee! In this committee you will learn useful organization and leadership skills by marketing the events hosted by SHPE and by capturing all the wonderful moments created in SHPE. You will learn how to efficiently advertise and market events through the use of social media, flyers, etc. This committee encourages you to step out of your comfort zone to form new bonds of friendship and networks. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me @ sacshpe.historian@gmail.com. Follow us on Instagram @Shpesac!

About Us

Mission

SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.

Vision

SHPE’s vision is a world where Hispanics are highly valued and influential as the leading innovators, scientists, mathematicians, and engineers.
